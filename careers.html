Our Workplace Culture

Our workplace culture challenges our employees, and rewards them for meeting and exceeding those challenges. As a result, we have realized long-term success and global growth, fueling our ongoing search for people who share our vision, our commitment to excellence and our values:

  • Safety
  • Customers first
  • Unyielding integrity
  • Local management and local control

Building a Career with
BrandSafway

We’re dedicated to helping you create a successful and fulfilling career path, regardless of where your journey starts. Whether you join our team as a scaffold carpenter, project controls manager or sales representative, we’re committed to helping you explore career paths that will help you reach your goals.

Why Choose BrandSafway?

When you begin your career with BrandSafway, you become part of our growing success. Through our employees, we’re able to deliver differentiated value to our customers by offering the highest level of productivity, industry-leading depth of experience and the broadest range of solutions, all backed by an unwavering commitment to safety. We’re focused on our customers’ success, which is why we prioritize customer service and focus on maintaining strong local relationships but we can’t do this without our talented and dedicated team.
