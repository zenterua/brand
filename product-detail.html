Features and Benefits

QuikDeck's patented configuration can adapt to fit most shapes and sizes — making it the ideal solution for a variety of projects.

For more information visit quikdeck.com

  • Components can be easily handled by one person.
  • Requires no special tools or skills to assemble.
  • Load capacities range from 25 psf to more than 75 psf.
  • Can be built in the air or on the ground and then hoisted into position.
  • Equipment can be leapfrogged and relocated as work progresses.
